Electrical

•Accurate Sensors: Equipped with a pre-calibrated electrochemical sensor for formaldehyde and a professional laser scattering sensor for PM2.5, the DUST ALERT provides precise and reliable readings.

•Real-time Display: A large, clear display with 3-color indicator lights allows you to easily see the air quality at a glance.

•Rechargeable Battery: The DUST ALERT comes with a built-in rechargeable battery that lasts for up to 8 hours on a single charge.

• PM2.5: Particulate matter with a diameter of 2.5 micrometers or less, which can be harmful to your health.

•HCHO: Formaldehyde, a colorless and odorless gas that can cause irritation to the eyes, nose, and throat.

• TVOCs: Total volatile organic compounds, which are emitted from a variety of sources, including paints, solvents, and cleaning products.

•AQI: Air Quality Index, a single number that provides an overall measure of air quality.

•Compact and Portable: Small and lightweight, the DUST ALERT is easy to carry and use anywhere you need to monitor air quality.

•Audible Alarm: The DUST ALERT can be set to sound an alarm when the air quality reaches a certain level.

•Multiple Applications: The DUST ALERT is ideal for use in homes, offices, schools, cars, and any other indoor environment.

Health

•Improve your health: By being aware of the air quality in your environment, you can take steps to improve it and protect your health.

•Increase productivity: Poor air quality can lead to fatigue, headaches, and other symptoms that can affect your productivity.

•Save money: By reducing the amount of time you spend in polluted environments, you can save money on energy bills and medical expenses.

•Protect your family: Children and pets are especially vulnerable to the effects of air pollution. The DUST ALERT can help you ensure that your family is breathing clean air.
